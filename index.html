Three Phases to Mastery

COURSE STRUCTURE
🏗
Days 1–30

Phase 1: Fundamentals

Build a rock-solid foundation in distributed systems, networking, storage, and reliability patterns.

  • Scalability & Load Balancing
  • Caching Strategies
  • SQL vs NoSQL & CAP Theorem
  • Replication & Sharding
  • Messaging & Event-Driven Architecture
  • Fault Tolerance & Observability
  • Authentication, Security & Rate Limiting
⚡
Days 31–60

Phase 2: Intermediate Systems

Master distributed algorithms and design 20+ classic systems that appear in real interviews.

  • Consensus: Paxos & Raft
  • Distributed Transactions & Sagas
  • Design URL Shortener, Chat, Feed
  • Design YouTube, Dropbox, Twitter
  • Cassandra, Kafka, Elasticsearch
  • Stream & Batch Processing
  • Advanced Storage Systems
🚀
Days 61–90

Phase 3: Principal Level

Advanced architecture patterns, infrastructure mastery, and structured interview preparation.

  • Kubernetes & Multi-Region Architecture
  • Design Uber, Google Maps, Snowflake
  • CQRS, Event Sourcing, DDD
  • Capacity Planning & Cost Optimization
  • Interview Framework & Estimation
  • Mock Interviews & Practice
  • Common Mistakes & Final Review
